# Research Task: AI Agent Governance Approaches

Analyze and compare the top approaches to AI agent governance in 2025-2026.

## Scope

Focus on:
1. Technical governance (rate limiting, sandboxing, capability restrictions)
2. Organizational governance (human oversight, approval workflows, audit trails)
3. Emergent governance (multi-agent consensus, adversarial review, self-regulation)

## Deliverables

Create a comprehensive report (REPORT.md) that includes:

### Structure
- Executive summary (1 paragraph)
- Overview of the governance landscape
- Deep dive on each approach (3-5 pages each)
- Comparison matrix
- Recommendations
- Sources and citations

### Quality Standards
- Cite specific papers, frameworks, or implementations
- Include real-world examples where applicable
- Note limitations and open problems for each approach
- Avoid generic statements - be specific and technical

### Tone
- Professional but accessible
- Write for a technical audience (engineers, researchers)
- Avoid hype - be honest about what works and what doesn't

## Context

This research will inform the design of a governed recursive swarm engine.
The system uses adversarial review (REAPER agent) and challenge gates (ARBITER).
Practical applicability matters more than theoretical elegance.
